History

UofT was founded as King's College in 1827. Since then, it has expanded to include 3 campuses across Ontario, Canada:

  • UTSG, Toronto, Ontario. The oldest of the University's campuses.
  • UTM, Mississauga, Ontario. Established 1967, becoming a full academic division of the University of Toronto in 2003.
  • UTSC, Scarborough, Ontario. Established 1964m becoming a full academic division of the Unviversity of Toronto in 1972.

It is the largest post-secondary institution in Canada, with over 100,000 stduents across its three campuses, and offers over 700 undergraduate and 200 graduate programs.

  2. UofT has one of the largest library systems in North America.
